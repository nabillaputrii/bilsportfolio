PROJECT OVERVIEW

This competition has the theme "Grow Up Your Skills and Create Innovation with Technology". We decided to create a UI/UX design for mobile application-based car rental as a technological innovation according to the theme of the competition. We use the design thinking method to make application prototypes. For the empathy process and defining the problem, the process is the same as my previous project, which can be checked on this link. We conducted interviews and research with actual users who faced the same problems related to the car rental process. After that, ideate and prototype processes were carried out by creating an interface design layout that was different from the previous project.
